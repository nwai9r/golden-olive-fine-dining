About Us

At Golden Olive Fine Dining we believe everyone deserves a luxury dining experience. Our restaurant combines locally sourced UAE produce with carefully selected premium ingredients from around the world. By using affordable local tomatoes and cucumbers we keep our prices reasonable while maintaining exceptional quality and profitability. Our mission is to offer fine dining that is accessible to families students and professionals throughout the UAE.

Economics Analysis

Natural Resources

Tomatoes cucumbers herbs chicken beef salmon and other ingredients used to prepare meals.


Human Resources

Chefs servers managers cashiers and kitchen staff who prepare and serve food.


Capital Resources

Kitchen equipment ovens refrigerators tables chairs computers and the restaurant building.


Scarcity Tradeoffs and Opportunity Cost

Due to higher transportation costs imported ingredients have become more expensive. Golden Olive Fine Dining uses locally available tomatoes and cucumbers because they are abundant in the UAE and cost less than many imported products. This helps the restaurant keep prices affordable while maintaining profitability. If the restaurant used expensive imported ingredients in every dish prices would need to increase. Using local produce allows the restaurant to attract more customers while controlling costs.
